About Our Foundation

Zelly Memorial Foundation was established to honor the legacy of our beloved educator. Our mission is to continue her work and ensure that every child has access to quality education, irrespective of their background. We believe that education is the key to a brighter future and will strive to make a positive impact on the lives of children.

"Her passion for education was contagious. She touched the lives of countless children and inspired them to dream big. She will forever be in our hearts."
A Loving, Supportive & Nurturing Parent
