Eulogy of Mama Zelly

Zelly Kasoha Lijondo

A woman after God's own heart. A loving wife, mother, sister, friend and grandmother.

About the Legend

☛ BIRTH The late Zelly Kasoha Lijondo was born on 14th April 1960 in Gavudi Village Lusengeli sublocation Wodanga location Sabatia Subcounty Vihiga County. ·She was the 7th child in the family of the late Joseph Lijondo and the late Respah Ayuma. She was of Vakitsungu clan uncled to Vamagetsa Vasari. She was a sister to Ben, Christopher, Henry, Wilson, Rose and Janet who are all alive.

☛ DEDICATION She was dedicated in Chamakambi PAG church by Rev. Elisha Muyaga on 10th May 1961.

☛ EDUCATION She joined Kapsambo pre-primary school in 1967. She then joined class one in the same school in 1968 until 1975 when she sat for her C.P.E and passed well. ·She joined Busali Union Secondary School from 1976 to 1979 when she sat for her C.S.E and passed well. ·She then joined Kenya Polytechnic where she pursued a computer course.

☛ EMPLOYMENT She started pursuing her career with the Ministry of Labour in 1982 as a data machine operator in Nairobi. She later joined National Social Security Fund (NSSF) which was a department of Ministry of Labour, in 1989. She then transferred to Kakamega branch and retired on April 14th 2020.

☛ MARRIAGE She got married to Elekia Ajanga Mung’asia under customary marriage on 23rd July 1983, later the marriage was registered officially. The marriage was blessed with 4 children: Hilda, Michelle, Lennox and Ivy. She is also blessed with 7 grand children.

☛ CHRISTIANITY She was baptized in water by Rev. Habily Musivogi on 11th August 1974 in Chamakambi-Ikumba Assembly Givudianyi. ·When she came to Kakamega, she joined Shikhambi PAG Assembly and later joined Maraba PAG where she was a member until her death.

☛ SICKNESS She suffered a stroke in 1999 where she was diagnosed with Rheumatic heart disease and was operated on in the Mater Hospital Nairobi, she healed and managed well the condition for years. ·Unfortunately, she had a recurrent stroke on 15th July 2018 and was treated at MTRH Eldoret, Aga Khan Hospital and Lumino Nursing Home where she recovered after few months. ·She had a third stroke on 28th May 2022 where she attended various hospitals with her main physician being Dr. Alliboy at the Aga Khan hospital Kisumu. ·She took her last breath at her home in Maraba, Kakamega surrounded by family. May her soul rest in peace.

☛ “A woman of power.”
